HABTECH ROBOTICS 1.1
A mod for Kerbal Space Program 1.12.x by Benjee10. 

INSTALLATION
Place the contents of GameData/ into your KSP/GameData/ directory. 

DEPENDENCIES
- B9PartSwitch
